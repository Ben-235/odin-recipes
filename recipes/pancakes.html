Breakfast Pancakes

Ever had the urge to have some tasty pancakes on a Saturday
morning but have no idea on where to start? Well you are in luck
because at Odin Recipes we have the best recipes ranging from lunch
,breakfast or supper. Let's get into making the best pancakes.

Ingridients

  • 2 cups whole milk
  • 2 and a half cups of vinegar
  • 3/4 cup of sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 3 large eggs

Steps

  1. Preheat your pan to 175 degrees celsius
  2. Whisk together flour, sugar, baking powder
    and salt in a bowl
  3. Beat eggs into a large bowl untill smooth.
    Add milk and whisk, add flour mixture and whisk
    let rest for 3 minutes and stir again
  4. Melt some butter on your pan, wipe with a paper
    toel, carefully. Spoon butter 1/3 cup portions onto
    the pan.
  5. Flip pancakes until brown on each side. Serve
    immediately with butter and toppings
