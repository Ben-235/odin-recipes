Grillled Tri-tip Beef

Ingridients

  • 2 kilograms tri-tip toast beef
  • 4 cloves garlic, prepared
  • 1/3 cup salt
  • 1/3 cup black pepper

Steps

  1. Using a knife, cut small slits in the top of the roast
    insert garlic slices into the slits.
  2. Mix salt, pepper and garlic slat together in a small bowl
    rub all over the tri-tip and refrigerate for atleast 1 hr to a day.
    remove the tri-tip from the fridge abt 20 mins before grilling
  3. Pregeat an outdoor grill for hight heat and lightly oil the grate
  4. Place the meat directly above the flame to sear the meat
    and lock in the juices, abt 5 to 10minutes per side.
  5. Turn the grill down to medium heat and continue to cook
    turning occasionally for another 25 to 30 mins.
