Apple, Strawberry and Banana Smoothie

Ingridients

  • 3/4 cup vanilla almond milk
  • 1 apple
  • 1 cup strawberries
  • 1 banana
  • 1/4 cup vanilla greek yogurt
    • Fruits should be peeled and cut into small pieces

Steps

  1. Place almond milk, apple, strawberries, banana and yogurt
    in a blender. Process untill smooth.
  2. Pour into serving glasses and serve immediately.
